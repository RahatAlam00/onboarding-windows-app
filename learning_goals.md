# Learning Goals Reflection



## What do you hope to learn from this internship?



I hope to gain practical software development experience by working on a real-world application. I want to understand how professional software teams collaborate, manage projects, review code, and deliver features. I also hope to improve my understanding of C#, WPF development, Git, and software engineering best practices.



## What skills are you most excited to develop?



I am most excited to develop my programming skills, problem-solving abilities, debugging techniques, and software design knowledge. I am also interested in improving my understanding of desktop application development, clean code practices, and professional development workflows using Git and GitHub.



## What do you think you can contribute to the team?



I can contribute enthusiasm, a willingness to learn, and a strong work ethic. I am committed to completing tasks, learning from feedback, and continuously improving my technical skills. I can also contribute a fresh perspective as a student who is eager to apply academic knowledge to real-world projects.



## What is one area you feel less confident in that you'd like to improve?



I currently feel less confident in my coding and problem-solving abilities, especially when working independently on unfamiliar tasks. Through this internship, I would like to become more confident in designing solutions, writing code independently, and understanding larger software projects.



## What does success in this internship look like for you?



Success in this internship would mean significantly improving my technical skills, becoming more confident as a software developer, contributing meaningful work to the project, and gaining practical industry experience that prepares me for a graduate software engineering role after completing my degree.
