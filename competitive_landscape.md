# Competitive Landscape Reflection

## What makes Focus Bear different from these apps?

Focus Bear combines habit building, distraction blocking, focus sessions, break reminders, and routine management in a single application. Freedom and Cold Turkey mainly focus on blocking distractions, while Tiimo and Routinery focus more on routines, planning, and task guidance. Focus Bear attempts to bridge both areas by helping users build productive habits while also protecting their focus. Another distinguishing factor is its emphasis on supporting people with ADHD, Autism, and executive functioning challenges.

## If you were a user, why would you choose Focus Bear over competitors?

If I wanted a single platform to help me stay focused and maintain healthy routines, I would choose Focus Bear. Instead of installing separate applications for blocking distractions and managing routines, Focus Bear provides both functions in one place. I also like that it encourages sustainable productivity through breaks and habit building rather than simply restricting access to distracting websites.

## What’s one feature that other apps have that Focus Bear doesn’t?

One feature that stands out is Tiimo's visual planning and scheduling system. Tiimo provides highly visual timelines and task planning tools that can make it easier for users to understand and organize their day at a glance. This visual approach may be particularly helpful for users who struggle with planning and time management.

## Based on your research, what’s one improvement you think Focus Bear could make?

One improvement Focus Bear could make is introducing a more visual daily planning dashboard. A timeline-based schedule that displays routines, focus sessions, breaks, and tasks in a single view could improve the user experience. This would help users better understand how their day is structured and make the app more appealing to people who prefer visual planning tools.
