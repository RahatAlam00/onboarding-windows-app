# How I Work Best



### I do my best work when



* Expectations and requirements are clearly defined.

* Tasks are broken down into manageable steps.

* I have enough context to understand the purpose of the task.

* I can ask questions when I get stuck.

* I can focus on one task at a time.



### I struggle when



* Requirements are vague or incomplete.

* There are too many context switches between tasks.

* I don't understand the reasoning behind a decision.

* I am expected to know a process without documentation or examples.
