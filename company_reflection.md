\# Company Reflection – Focus Bear



\## Why do you think Focus Bear was created?



I believe Focus Bear was created because the founders experienced the challenges of maintaining focus, building routines, and avoiding distractions, especially for people with ADHD. Traditional productivity apps often rely on reminders and self-discipline, which may not be enough for people who struggle with executive functioning. Focus Bear was built to provide more active support to boost productivity through routines, distraction blocking, and accountability.



\## What problem is Focus Bear solving?



Focus Bear helps people overcome distractions, procrastination, poor routines, and difficulty maintaining focus. The app guides users through helping build healthy habits, blocks distracting websites and applications, encourages productive breaks, and helps users stay on task across multiple devices. It is particularly designed bases on scientific research to support people with ADHD but can benefit anyone who struggles with productivity and time management.



\## Why do you think this mission is important?



In today's world, people face constant distractions from social media, notifications, and digital devices. These distractions can reduce productivity, increase stress, and make it difficult to build healthy habits. Helping people manage their attention and create sustainable routines can improve both their work performance and overall well-being. Focus Bear's mission is important because it addresses a challenge that affects almost every students, professionals, and many neurodivergent individuals.



\## How does Focus Bear’s work align with your personal values or interests?



As a Software Engineering student, I am interested in building technology that solves real-world problems and improves people's lives. Focus Bear combines software development, productivity, behavioral science, and accessibility to create a practical solution for users. In my last semester, I worked on a project to solve the same real=world problem that Focus Bear aims to achieve. I value technology that has a positive impact on people's daily lives, and Focus Bear's approach aligns with that goal.



\## Do you personally relate to any of the challenges that Focus Bear aims to solve?



Yes. As a student, I have experienced challenges with maintaining focus, avoiding distractions, and staying consistent with routines while balancing coursework, projects, internship responsibilities, and personal commitments. I understand how easy it is to become distracted by social media and lose productivity because I face this problem in my personal life as well. Because of this, I can relate to the problems that Focus Bear aims to solve and appreciate the value of tools that encourage better habits and focus.
